AI Based Rockfall Prediction

An AI-powered system that uses multi-source data to provide real-time rockfall predictions for open-pit mines.

The Problem

Rockfalls in open-pit mines pose a significant threat to personnel and equipment, resulting in injuries, operational delays, and financial loss. Traditional detection methods are often reactive and fail to provide predictive insights for proactive decision-making.

Our Solution

We have developed a smart, AI-based system capable of predicting potential rockfall incidents. The system processes multi-source data inputs to identify patterns that precede rockfall events. Our user-friendly dashboard provides real-time risk maps and probability-based forecasts to mine planners.
